# Tibia Vocation Guide for Mobile Players

Choosing a [vocation](/wiki/vocations) in Tibia is a permanent decision that shapes your entire gameplay experience. When playing on mobile, some vocations are easier to manage on a touchscreen than others. Here's everything you need to know.

## The Four Vocations

### Knight — The Tank

Knights are melee fighters with the highest HP in the game. They use swords, axes, or clubs and stand face-to-face with monsters.

**Strengths:**
- Highest health pool — hardest to kill
- Simple combat: walk up and attack
- Can wear the heaviest armor
- Great for solo play

**On Mobile:**
Knights are the **best vocation for mobile play**. Melee combat is straightforward on a touchscreen — tap a monster, stand next to it, and attack. No kiting, no aiming, no complex spell rotations.

### Paladin — The Ranger

Paladins use ranged weapons (bows, crossbows) and have a balance of HP and mana. They attack from a distance and can use some healing spells.

**Strengths:**
- Ranged attacks — fight from safety
- Good health and mana
- Very profitable (save money on potions)
- Can hunt efficiently solo

**On Mobile:**
Paladins work well on mobile. Distance fighting means you take less damage, and the simpler movement patterns make touchscreen play comfortable. Just maintain distance and shoot.

### Sorcerer — The Nuker

Sorcerers deal the most damage in Tibia with powerful elemental magic. They have low HP but devastating area spells.

**Strengths:**
- Highest damage output
- Area spells clear groups fast
- Elemental magic (fire, ice, earth, energy)
- Fast leveling with the right hunts

**On Mobile:**
Sorcerers are **challenging on mobile**. Effective sorcerer play requires precise positioning for area spells and quick reactions to avoid death. Your low HP means mistakes are punishing. Recommended for experienced players.

### Druid — The Healer

Druids are the healers and support class of Tibia. They use nature and ice magic for damage, and have the strongest healing spells in the game.

**Strengths:**
- Best healing in the game
- Essential for team hunts
- Ice and earth damage spells
- Always wanted in groups

**On Mobile:**
Druids are **moderately challenging on mobile**. Solo hunting is straightforward (similar to sorcerer), but team healing requires fast targeting which can be tricky on a touchscreen. Great for solo play, takes practice for team hunts.

## Mobile Vocation Tier List

From easiest to hardest on mobile:

1. **Knight** — Simple and forgiving. Best for mobile beginners.
2. **Paladin** — Comfortable ranged play. Good balance.
3. **Druid** — Fine solo, challenging in team healer role.
4. **Sorcerer** — High skill ceiling. Rewarding but demanding on touch.

## Tips for Any Vocation on Mobile

- **Hotkey your most-used [spells](/wiki/spells)** — set up the spell bar before you hunt
- **Bring extra supplies** — you can't micromanage inventory easily on mobile
- **Use mana potions generously** — running out of mana on mobile is dangerous
- **Know your escape route** — before entering a spawn, know how to get out
- **Practice in safe zones** — get comfortable with the controls before dangerous hunts
