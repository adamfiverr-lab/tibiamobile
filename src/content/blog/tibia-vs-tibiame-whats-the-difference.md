# Tibia vs TibiaME: What's the Difference?

If you've searched for "Tibia mobile" you've probably found two different things: **TibiaME** and the original **Tibia**. They share a name and a developer (CipSoft), but they're very different games. Here's everything you need to know.

## TibiaME: CipSoft's Mobile Spinoff

TibiaME (Tibia Micro Edition) launched in 2003 as a separate game designed specifically for mobile devices. At the time, phones couldn't handle anything close to the full Tibia experience, so CipSoft created a simplified version.

**TibiaME features:**
- Completely different world and map
- Simplified combat system
- Different monsters and quests
- Top-down 2D graphics (different art style from Tibia)
- Available on app stores
- Separate account system — your Tibia characters don't exist in TibiaME

## Classic Tibia: The Original MMORPG

Tibia is the original game that launched in 1997. It features a massive open world, complex economy, PvP systems, hundreds of quests, and a deep vocation system. For decades, it was desktop-only.

**Classic Tibia features:**
- Massive world with hundreds of cities, dungeons, and areas
- [Four vocations](/wiki/vocations): Knight, Paladin, Sorcerer, Druid
- Complex quest system
- Player-driven economy and trading
- PvP combat and guild wars
- 25+ years of content updates

## So How Do You Play Classic Tibia on Mobile?

This is where [Minibia](https://minibia.com) comes in. Minibia is a browser-based client that brings the full classic Tibia experience to your mobile device. It's not a spinoff or a simplified version — it's the real deal.

**With Minibia you get:**
- The same world, monsters, and items as classic Tibia
- Touch-optimized controls for mobile play
- No download required — runs in your browser
- Works on Android, iPhone, iPad, and desktop
- Free to play

## Side-by-Side Comparison

| Feature | Tibia (via Minibia) | TibiaME |
|---------|-------------------|---------|
| World | Classic Tibia world | Separate, smaller world |
| Vocations | Knight, Paladin, Sorcerer, Druid | Warrior, Mage |
| Depth | 25+ years of content | Simplified for mobile |
| Download | No — browser-based | App store download |
| Cost | Free | Free with in-app purchases |
| Platform | Any browser / any device | Android & iOS apps |

## Which Should You Play?

**Choose classic Tibia (Minibia) if:**
- You've played Tibia before and want the same experience on mobile
- You want a deep, complex MMORPG with decades of content
- You don't want to download an app
- You want a fully player-driven economy

**Choose TibiaME if:**
- You prefer a native mobile app experience
- You want a simpler, more casual MMORPG
- You don't care about playing the original Tibia specifically
